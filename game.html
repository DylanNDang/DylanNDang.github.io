Favorite Games I've played and HIGHLY Recommend

  • NieR: Automata
    • It's a Yoko Taro game.
    • Hot android characters.
    • But there's a lot of depression.
    • Watching every single characters suffer in the worst ways possible (especially Nines).
    • A painful lesson in nihilism and existentialism.
    • It makes you rethink about your entire existence as a human being.
  • Yakuza 0
    • Insanely good and compelling story.
    • Extremely well written characters (it's a shame that some of them had to die).
    • At the start you will hate the three Dojima family lieutenants, but after fighting them you start to feel for them and they earn your respect.
    • Way too many mini games and things to do.
    • Karaoke.
    • It's a serious crime drama I swear.
  • NieR Replicant ver.1.22474487139...
    • It's a Yoko Taro game.
    • If you want more depression.
    • You are the "hero" which means you are the good guy, but the game does not make you feel like the good guy.
    • You make some questionably moral decision, like destroying a entire town of innocent civilians just to save yourself and your friends.
    • The game makes you kills these bosses in the first route, and when you play the other routes and fight those bosses again, the game make you feel bad for killing them.
  • Yakuza: Like a Dragon
    • They really made it into a turn base RPG because of an April Fools joke.
    • Its like Dragon Quest but it takes place in modern day Japan.
    • Instead of having character classes, you have jobs that changes what your party members attacks and ability (it just makes sense).
    • Way too many mini games and things to do.
    • You can become the CEO of a billion yen company and still be homeless.
    • The abilities ranges from things you can see in a actual fight to things you WON'T see in a actual fight.
    • You can "summon" characters with the power of your cellphone like a Lobster to assist you in a street fight (there is a in game explanation for this system).
    • Yakuza is a serious crime drama.
  • Persona 5 Royal
    • If the Persona 5 was a 10/10 game then this version is like a 12/10.
    • You do things every normal Japanese high schoolers would do, like fight demons in another dimension.
    • It is a JPRG/Visual Novel/Dating Sim/School Life Simulator.
    • The protagonist literally gives zero fs though out the entire story.
    • Your cat makes you go to sleep even if you don't want to.
    • Way too many mini games and things to do, Sega really knows how to make mini games.
    • You can date one girl or two girls, or ALL of the girls.
